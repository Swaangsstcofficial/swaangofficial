All about Drama club

Swaang the Drama Club is designed for students interested in learning more about the aspects of theater. This includes learning stage terminology, working on acting skills, set building, and costumes/makeup design. Drama Club is open to all students. To promote communication skills, teamwork, socialize and self confidence.


The Dramatic Club encourages dramatic presentations by students in the form of skits, mime, plays etc
